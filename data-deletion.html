Data Deletion Policy – Take Notes

Take Notes does not collect or store any personal data on external servers.

All your notes are stored locally on your device. You can delete your notes using the in-app delete feature (by swiping left or long-pressing a note). If you wish to delete all notes and app data, you can simply uninstall the app.

No account or login is required to use the app, and we do not store any user-generated content on our servers.
