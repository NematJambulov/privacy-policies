Privacy Policy for Take Notes

Effective date: July 15, 2025

Take Notes (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Android application Take Notes.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or contact information). However, we use third-party services that may collect information automatically to help us improve our app and serve ads.

a. Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. This service may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • App usage data (such as which features are used and for how long)
  • Crash reports
  • Country (approximate location)

All data is anonymized and used only in aggregate.

b. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display in-app advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • App usage behavior
  • Approximate location (for ad targeting)

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

2. How We Use the Information

The data collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Analyze usage trends and feature popularity
  • Deliver relevant ads (through Google AdMob)

We do not access or store this data ourselves.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties, except with the third-party services mentioned above, which may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy & Terms

4. Data Retention

We do not store user data ourselves. Data collected by third-party services is retained and managed according to their policies.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

6. Your Choices

You can limit or opt out of personalized ads by:

  • Going to your device's Settings > Google > Ads
  • Enabling “Opt out of Ads Personalization”

You can also uninstall the app to stop all data collection.
